Homemade Pretzels

Recipe

These homemade pretzels are delicious and easy!

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cup of warm water
  • 2 and 1/4 teaspoons instant or active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar or granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter melted or slightly cool
  • 3 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour plus more for the work surface
  • coarse salt or coarse sea salt for sprinkling
