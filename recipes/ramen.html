Ramen Noodles

Recipe

These ramen noodles are a hit on college campuses!

Ingredients

  • Enough water to cook the noodles
  • A package of Maruchan ramen noodles

Steps

  1. Boil water in medium saucepan
  2. Remove flavor packet from package
  3. Add ramen to pan
  4. Cook for 3-5 minutes, stirring occasionally
  5. Drain, leaving a small amount of broth
  6. Add flavor packet and stir well
