Easy Kraft Mac n' Cheese

Recipe

This mac n' cheese is an easy addition to your dinner!

Ingredients

  • 6 cups water
  • 4 tablespoons butter or margarine
  • 1/4 cup milk

Steps

  1. Boil water in medium saucepan
  2. Add in macaroni and cook for 7-8 minutes, stirring occasionally
  3. Drain DO NOT RINSE then return to pan
  4. Add margarine or butter, milk, and cheese sauce mix
  5. Stir well
